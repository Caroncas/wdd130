Crans-Montana, Switzerland

Crans Montana is a beautiful little town nestled in the hills of Switzerland. It is a 2 hour drive from the city of Geneva and nearly 3 hours from the Matterhorn. While this small town may not seem worth the long detour, it is worth its own trip entirely.

The sites of Crans-Montana, similar to those found throughout Switzerland, are absolutely breathtaking. The air is clean, the light pollution is nonexistent, and the residents are among the kindest you'll ever meet.

Should you ever find yourself up in the hills of Crans-Montana, you will find yourself at home.
