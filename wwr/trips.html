All overnight rafting excursion packages include:

  • One of our certified river guides trained in First-Aid care and wilderness survival
  • Outdoor accomodations such as tents, restrooms, communal showers, and First Aid stations depending on the trip
  • Breakfast and dinner meals served aroud roughly 7 a.m. and 6 p.m.
  • Transportation to and from Dry Oars Rafting Company headquarters before and after your journey

*Extra information regarding individual preparations will be sent out one month prior to your rafting expidition.
If you have any questions regarding a reservation, please view the 'Contact Us' page.

*By Idaho State law and company policy, any group larger than 30 people requires a second river guide. In this case an extra fee of $250 per night will be added to the total.
